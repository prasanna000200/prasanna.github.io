HOW TO PLAY

1. Click start to play.

2. A random color will blink, click that color.

3. Next, a random color will blink, here, you need to click the colors you have clicked before, and click the color which blinked now.

4. The game continues until, you mess up the color sequence from the start.

5. Click start to play again.
